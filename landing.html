THE OBJECTIVES OF LEARNING OPERATING SYSTEMS

At the end of this lesson, learners should be able to;

  1. Give a detailed definition of Operating Systems.
  2. Understand the history, functions, features and components of Operating Systems
  3. Identify the types of Operating Systems.
  4. List examples of Operating Systems.
  5. Understand and explain the historical development of each of the most common OS.
